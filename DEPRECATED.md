* `springer-context`  
   Replaced by [`brand-context`](https://github.com/springernature/frontend-toolkits/tree/master/context)

* `springer-nature-context`  
   Replaced by [`brand-context`](https://github.com/springernature/frontend-toolkits/tree/master/context)
